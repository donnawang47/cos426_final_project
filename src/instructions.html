Late Meal

How to play:

Try to stay alive in the game longer than your opponent!

Instructions:

Player 1 use the 'wasd' keys to move backwards, left, forward, and right, and the spacebar to jump. Player 2 use the arrow keys to move in the four directions and the 'm' key to jump. Both players must avoid the moving tables and chairs in order to stay alive.

Click to start playing!
